Rail Pressure Relief Valve.

FRP PID Idle → Key-off

With the engine warm and idling, watch the FRP rail-pressure PID on the scan tool during a smooth key-off. Track the decay rate over the first ten seconds after shutdown.

A healthy sealed rail holds for tens of seconds. A stuck-open relief valve bleeds in two or three — which mimics a CP4.2 failure under load.
