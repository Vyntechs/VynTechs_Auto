Topology,
interactive.

The wiring topology is the diagnostic. Role-coded wires animate with directional power flow. Scenarios — Key off, Idle, Light load, Medium load, Heavy load, fault sims — re-animate the system so the tech sees what should be happening, then probes the truck and compares. Pin selection shows where to probe, the right-now reading for the current scenario, the expected range, and what wrong means.
